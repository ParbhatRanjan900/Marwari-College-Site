      ABOUT BCA

      BCA stands for Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) and it is an undergraduate 3 year degree course in computer applications who wish to delve into the world of Computer languages. Today we have seen a noticeable rapid growth of IT industry in India and this increasing growth has created a lot of opportunities for the computer graduates. The BCA course is one of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology. A degree in BCA is at par with a B.Tech/B.E degree in Computer Science or Information Technology. For a BCA aspirant, this degree would help in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Applications. A student can end up being very successful after completing the course. Moreover, after completing BCA the students can also go for Masters in the same course which is Masters in Computer Application (MCA), many can also opt for MBA (Masters In Business Administration). The duration of the course is 3 years and divided into 6 semesters. It comprises of the subjects like database, networking, data structure, core programming languages like ‘C’ and ‘java’. This course provides a lot of opportunities to the students who are interested in computer field and wants to work in the IT sector as programmer or software developer.


      WHY BCA?

      Bachleor of Computer Application(BCA) is one of the popular course among the students who wants to make their career in the Information Technology(IT) field. There is a huge scope in the field of computer and also self-employement option is available. You can do freelancing or develop your own software if you have that much skills. There are many software Multi National Companies(MNCs) which provide job to the BCA graduates. If candidate has a work experience and all the necessary required skills then he/she can hold good position in MNCs. With regard

      NUMBER OF SEATS

      The maximum number of seats in BCA is 100.
      Reservation of seats for the course shall be as per the rules of the State Govt. & T.M.B.U.,Bhagalpur.


      ELIGIBLITY CRITERIA

      • Minimum marks in Intermediate is 45%.
      • Passed out from Science stream which is Physics,Chemistry and Math.


      ADMISSION PROCEDURE

      Eligibility for admission:-

      • Minimum of 45% marks in Intermediate Examination for BCA with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics(Science).
      • List of candidate of Vocational/Professional Courses will be made on the basis of merit List prepared after the entrance Test Examination including written and vice-voice Examination conducted by the college.
      • The maximum number of seats of BCA is 100.
      • Reservation of seats for the course shall be as per the rules of the State Govt. & T.M.B.U.,Bhagalpur.

      How to apply:-
      • Candidate seeking admission to these coures at Marwari College during the academic session 2019-22 may obtain the prescribed Application Form from the college counter on cash payment of Rs. 500/-
      • The cost of Prospectus along with the Application Form for the course includes the Entrance Test Fee. No any other fee is required to be submitted at the time of Apllication Form.

      Submission of Application Form:-
      • Application of prescribed form complete in all respects should be submitted either in person or by post on or before due date.
      • The Application Fee is non-refundable and no correspondance in this subject will be entertained.
      • Two recent color (Passport size) photographs duly self attested should be affixed on the application Form.
      • Application Form must be filled in and duly signed by the applicant. Incomplete application form shall be summarily rejected.
      • Candidates whose results have not been declared, may also apply for admission provided they produce the result/certificate of the qualifying examination by the last date of admission as per the list in which the name of students figures. Any subsequent production of result certificate of the qualifying examination shall not vest in the candidate any right of admission.

      Entrance test details:-
      • The admission of students to the course shall be made on the basis of marks obtained by the candidates in Entrance Test Examination comprising of Writeen Examination & Interviews.
        • Physics: 25 marks
        • Chemistry: 25 marks
        • Mathematics: 25 marks
        • Career: 10 marks
        • Viva-Voce: 15 marks
        • Total(marks): 100 marks
      • All the candidates are require to appear at the Entrance Test and Interviews in accordance with the schedule. No intimation will be sent to be individual candidates. In case of change of admission schedule, if any, a notice will be put up on the notice board of the respective department in the college.
      • Candidate selected for interview are required to bring their Admit Card issued for Entrance Test and attested two copies along with the original of the following documents at the time of interview.
        • Marks Sheet and Admit Card of Intermediate Examination
        • Marks Sheet for Matric Examination
        • Migration Certificate
        • Caste Certificate for reserve category
        • Two color passport size photographs
